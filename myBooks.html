  • The Battles of Ekhelyra Series: Remember Me

    Back in the year 1507, the Kingdom of Ekhelyra was ruled by King Yumi Shinobu -- a super homophobic king who will burn any gay person a the stake -- and his wife: Queen Mariah Shinobu. His 6 year old son, Naiomi, has gotten pretty used to royal life. He likes being able to ask for anything and get it. He is freely allowed to explore the kingdom, as it is not that big and it's hard to get lost. Naiomi has a twin sister named Isabel. While everyone in the kingdom knows Naiomi, almost no one knows who she is. She doesn't care. She sees all of the newspaper people interviewing Naiomi and realizes that she doesn't want anything to do with that.
